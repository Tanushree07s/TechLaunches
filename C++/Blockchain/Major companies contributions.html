 Binance

  • It is the world's largest cryptocurrency exchange by daily trading volume The total value of all buy and sell transactions that happen on an exchange in one day.
  • BNB Chain is a blockchain created by Binance, and is optimized for smart contracts and decentralied apps.
  • Compared to Ethereum, BNB Chain offers much cheaper transactions, making it attractive for DeFi (Decentralized Finance) users.
  • The chain is built to be compatible with the Ethereum Virtual Machine (EVM) The Ethereum Virtual Machine (EVM) is the computation engine that executes smart contracts and manages the state of the Ethereum blockchain. , so the Ethereum-based smart contracts, tools, and dApps can run on BNB Chain with little to no modification.

  • Corporate Influence & Adoption:
    • TravelbyBit & Travala : Allow people to pay for flights and hotels using BNB, bringing crypto into real-world travel.
